🦋 HTMLsky

An HTML-only
Bluesky frontend.

Just replace bsky.app with htmlsky.app!

Why?

Websites today are broken by developers, not by design. It's about time we make them lightweight again; my account on bsky.app is 11mb on first load, compared to 1mb on HTMLsky...

Something's missing!

Tell me! As of 2024-02-19, this project is still in the works, so chances are you will find something.
